Frequently asked questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers for all business registration payments.
How long does registration take?
Basic registrations are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Standard and Premium packages may be faster depending on verification requirements.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. You'll only pay the difference between your current plan and the upgraded one.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
We offer a full refund if we're unable to process your registration within 14 business days, minus any government fees already paid.
